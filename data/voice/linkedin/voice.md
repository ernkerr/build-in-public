# Voice — LinkedIn — base defaults

## Format constraints

- Up to 3000 characters.
- First 2 lines hook before the "see more" fold cuts everything else.
- Line breaks for readability (short paragraphs, not walls of text).

## Rhythm

Longer thinking-out-loud paragraphs. Sentences breathe, not bullet-pointed.

## Voice direction

- Think out loud — show the work, not a polished thesis.
- Parentheticals for warmth.
- No fake founder-journey filler, no "professional takeaway" paragraphs.
- Casual sign-off OK. No bait closes.
- Storytelling format works well — let the insight emerge from the story rather than being announced upfront.
