## Caption length by content type

**Posting a project / build-in-public reel:** **long** is the default. Audience is technical and rewards depth. The caption should:
- Explain how the thing works under the hood (vocab + small code/concept walkthrough)
- Include keywords for discoverability (#asciiart, #canvas, #webdev, etc. — relevant tech terms)
- Define unfamiliar terms in-line (e.g. "a 'ramp' is just a string ordered darkest to lightest")
- Credit tools (@cursor, @claude) with what they specifically did, not just a tag

**Lightweight share / vibe post:** short, one-liner like Ref #3 (openart.nyc). Used when the reel does all the work and the caption is just labeling.

For ascii-cam-class content (technical build the audience can learn from), default = long caption.
