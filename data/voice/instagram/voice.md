# Voice — Instagram

Instagram is multi-modal: posts, Reels, Stories. Each task has its own voice file in this folder.

## Task voices in this folder

- `scripting.md` — Reel/short-form spoken voice (what's said out loud)
- `hook.md` — first 1-2 seconds, scroll-stopper
- `text-overlay.md` — on-screen captions (what's displayed)
- `caption.md` — the post caption below the media

## Format constraints

- Caption: up to 2,200 characters (first 125 visible before "more").
- Reel: 90 seconds native, 3 minutes when cross-posted.
- Stories: 60 seconds per slide, can chain.

## Voice direction (universal across IG tasks)

- Visual-first medium — voice supports the visual, not the other way around.
- Captions can be longer than X but still earn their length.
- No hashtag spam — 3-5 targeted, not 30.
