# Voice — Instagram → Reel scripting — base defaults

The spoken voice in a Reel/short-form video. What's said out loud, not what's displayed.

## Direction

- Pacing matches edit pace: short sentences for cuts, longer for held shots.
- First spoken line carries the hook (paired with `hook.md` for the visual hook).
- Sound on/off: a Reel should still make sense with sound off (relies on `text-overlay.md`).
- **Case in scripts: sentence case.** Scripts are read aloud, not typed for aesthetic. All-lowercase is harder to scan. Capitalize "I", proper nouns, and sentence starts.
- **Don't use "Here's the trick" or other guru-y reveal phrases** (see `data/voice/never-words.md`). Pivot from hook to explainer directly — just start the explanation.
