# Voice — X

## Rhythm

Short fragments with line breaks for pacing. Standalone lines, no terminal periods. Punchy meme cadence at the extreme (*"RAAS"*, *"cook or be cooked"*) — one-line posts that work as standalone declarations.

## Format constraints

- 280 characters per tweet.
- Threads: up to 10 tweets, separated by `---`. Only when one tweet would collapse the argument. Each tweet must advance.

## Voice direction

- Hook is the first line.
- Very compressed. Cut every word that doesn't earn its place.
- 1–2 hashtags max. `#buildinpublic` is the default.
- Drop terminal periods on standalone short lines.
- Single emoji at end is OK, not required.
