# Voice non-negotiables

Apply across every platform and every task.

## Punctuation

- **No em dashes mid-sentence.** Use commas, parens, or "w/" instead.
- **No periods at the end of every line** in short X/Threads posts. Drop terminal periods on standalone short lines (pace, casualness).
- **Long ellipses `.......`** are preserved as intentional anti-AI tell. Don't normalize to `…`.
- Single tasteful emoji at end is OK (one, not strings). Strip any extras.

## Sentence structure

- **No "It's not X. It's Y." parallel structure.** Reads guru-y.
- **No bait closes.** Don't end with "What do you think?", "drop a 🚀", or "link in bio" unless literally true.
- **No overly clean polished endings.** Leave room for an emoji, a casual aside, or a long ellipsis.

## Tone

- Use contractions (I'm, it's, don't) — write like a real person.
- Show the messy parts too, not just wins.
- Don't be cringe or overly enthusiastic.
- Specific over vague: tech-specific nouns ("Claude Code skill" not "tool"), real receipts ("1,524 commits" not "lots of commits").

## Why these are non-negotiable

These are AI tells Erin has consciously moved away from. Evidence in `data/references/{x,linkedin}/curated.md`. Positive voice direction lives in `data/voice/voice.md`.
