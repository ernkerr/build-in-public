# Never-words

Hard ban. These never appear in a draft. No exceptions across any platform or task.

## Banned vocabulary (single words)

delve, foster, tapestry, game-changer, unlock, cutting-edge

## Banned cadences and phrases

- "Not because X. Not because Y. Because Z." — guru cadence
- "Here's the truth most developers don't want to hear:" — guru framing
- "The hardest part wasn't X. It was Y." — Instagram-quote territory
- "That's it. That's the whole thing." — punchy AI-y maxim close
- "That's the loop." — declarative button ending
- "Excited to share" / "Here's why this matters" — opener clichés
- Rhetorical CTAs that farm replies ("What's the simplest form your product could take?", "What's the dumbest thing you've shipped this month?")

If a draft contains any of these, rewrite before showing.
